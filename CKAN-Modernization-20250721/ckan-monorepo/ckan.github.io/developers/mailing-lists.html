Mailing lists

As an open source project we have active mailing lists where product and development discussions and decisions are made. To post to either mailing list, you first need to join the list by signing up on the pages below. You can also look through the archive of previous messages from there.

  • CKAN developer mailing list – primarily focused on technical issues, questions, problems and bugs: http://lists.okfn.org/mailman/listinfo/ckan-dev
  • CKAN discussion mailing list – for non-technical discussions about features, future plans and news: http://lists.okfn.org/mailman/listinfo/ckan-discuss
